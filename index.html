About Our Company

Concerted Efforts To Build Better

Santosh Polymers is a leading manufacturer and supplier of Oleochemicals, Crop Extracts, Essential Oils, and eco-friendly Packaging products in India.

We started our journey in the year 2012 by trading industrial chemicals. Recognizing the need for sustainable development, we recently ventured into high-tech manufacturing, extracting organic chemicals from industrial leftovers and creating moulded pulp trays to replace traditional packaging.
