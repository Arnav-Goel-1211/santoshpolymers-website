Paint & Coating Industry

Oleo chemicals (specifically distilled soya fatty acid and oleic acid) serve as primary raw materials and additives that aid in paint synthesis, film drying, gloss formation, and coating lifespan enhancement.

Bio Diesel Industry

We supply distilled palm fatty acids and other organic by-products that serve as the main feedstocks for manufacturing fatty acid methyl esters (FAME), contributing to sustainable green energy development.

Pharmaceutical & Cosmetics

In pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and cosmetic formulations, our high-purity oleochemical esters and extracts serve as preservatives, thickening agents, emollients, and active ingredients.

Petrochemical Industry

Our bio-based alternatives are increasingly replacing traditional petroleum-based solvents and lubricating additives in industrial cleaning, metal machining, and oil/gas drilling fluids.

Resins & Plastics Industry

Our chemicals act as mould-release agents, flow aids, and plasticizers in rubber and plastics processing, as well as hydrophobic sizing agents in construction polymers.

Other Industries

We also supply key ingredients and packaging to: Speciality Chemicals manufacture, Food Organics, Animal/Poultry Feed formulation, and Industrial / Household Cleaning solutions.
