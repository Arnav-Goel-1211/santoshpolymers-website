About Santosh Polymers

Pioneering oleochemical manufacturing and sustainable packaging solutions since 1997 from Kurukshetra, Haryana.

Established 1997 • Kurukshetra, Haryana
Our Background

A Journey of Quality & Sustainability

Santosh Polymers was established with a focus on manufacturing and supplying high-quality industrial chemicals. Over the years, we have expanded our capacity to become a leading Indian supplier of bio-based oleochemicals and custom packaging solutions.

With an active manufacturing unit at Ladwa, Kurukshetra, Haryana, we cater to major industrial accounts PAN India. Our state-of-the-art labs and manufacturing infrastructure allow us to maintain 99%+ product purity and reliable bulk deliveries.

Our Growth

Milestones & History

1997

Packaging Products Unit Setup

We setup our first corrugated packaging unit at Ladwa, pioneering the replacement of wooden boxes for fruit/vegetable packaging in the region, preserving forest resources.

2002

Paper Chemicals Unit Expansion

We launched our specialty paper chemicals manufacturing unit at Poanta Sahib, introducing eco-friendly sizing agents that match international standards.

2009

Stepping Into Oleochemicals

We entered the oleochemicals sector, introducing high-purity vegetable-based fatty acids, soya lecithin, and specialty oils to satisfy growing national demand.

2021-Present

Construction Solutions Launch

We launched a complete range of epoxy resins, grouts, and tile fixing solutions, catering to the rapidly expanding infrastructure development segment in India.
