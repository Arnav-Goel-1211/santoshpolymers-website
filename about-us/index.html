Our Growth

Milestones & History

1997

Packaging Products Unit

Setup first corrugated packaging unit at Ladwa, pioneering the replacement of wooden boxes for fruit and vegetable packaging in the region.

2002

Paper Chemicals Expansion

Launched specialty paper chemicals manufacturing at Poanta Sahib, introducing eco-friendly sizing agents that match international quality standards.

2009

Entry Into Oleochemicals

Entered the oleochemicals sector with high-purity vegetable-based fatty acids, soya lecithin, and specialty oils to satisfy growing national demand.

2012

Santosh Polymers Founded

Launched dedicated oleochemicals and specialty chemicals manufacturing under the Santosh brand, marking a new chapter in industrial chemical production.

2017

Surfactants & Lubricants

Expanded into surfactants, lubricants, and bio-based industrial additives, serving the growing demand for sustainable alternatives in industrial processing.

2021

Construction Solutions

Launched a complete range of epoxy resins, grouts, and tile fixing solutions for the rapidly expanding infrastructure development segment in India.
